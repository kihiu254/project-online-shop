Returns & Exchanges

Our Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your Luna Luxe purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we offer a simple returns and exchanges process.

Return Window

  • 30-day return window from the date of delivery
  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and with original tags attached
  • Original packaging should be intact

How to Return

  1. Log into your Luna Luxe account
  2. Go to your order history
  3. Select the item(s) you wish to return
  4. Choose between a refund or exchange
  5. Print your return label or schedule a pickup

Exchange Process

Free Exchanges

  • Size exchanges are free within Kenya
  • Different color/style exchanges are treated as a return and new purchase
  • Exchange items are shipped as soon as we receive your return

Refund Information

  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  • Original shipping charges are non-refundable
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method

Non-Returnable Items

  • Intimate apparel and swimwear for hygiene reasons
  • Sale items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Custom or personalized items
  • Items damaged due to customer misuse

Need Help?

Our customer service team is here to assist you with returns and exchanges.
