Delivery Options

Standard Shipping (Within Kenya)

  • Delivery within 3-5 business days
  • Free shipping on orders over KES 5,000
  • Standard rate: KES 300

Express Shipping

  • Next-day delivery within Nairobi
  • 2-3 business days for other regions
  • Express rate: KES 500

International Shipping

  • Delivery within 7-14 business days
  • Rates calculated at checkout based on destination
  • Customs and import duties may apply

Tracking Your Order

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can track your order:

  • Through your Luna Luxe account
  • Using our Order Tracking page
  • Via the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email

Important Information

  • Orders are processed within 24-48 hours
  • Delivery times start from when your order ships
  • We deliver Monday through Saturday
  • Contact our support team for special delivery requests
